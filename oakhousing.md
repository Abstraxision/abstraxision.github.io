# Bay Area Housing Search

## Housing Posts Criteria 
> What to look for in apartment postings to discern legitimacy
1. A lot of photos or videos
2. A good description of the place that considers what. Person looking for housing would want that includes public transit nearby
3. You want to meet the person/video call and it’s a good sign if they want to do that. 
4. Not a high security deposit
5. Look for reviews. Reviews indicate that the place is very good. 

## Websites to Search for Housing
1. [Facebook Marketplace](https://www.facebook.com/marketplace/)
2. [Furnished Finder](https://www.furnishedfinder.com/)
3. [Craigslist](https://sfbay.craigslist.org/search/oakland-ca)
